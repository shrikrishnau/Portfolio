ABOUT ME

An aspiring Data Analyst with a highly collaborative and driven mindset. I like to find patterns through data and derive a unique perspective from it.

As an Analyst, I love communicating with data to derive meaningful insights to solve a particular problem. Whilst at Crafeed, I supported the Customer and Marketing Analysis team to improve ad revenue and found success in it.
